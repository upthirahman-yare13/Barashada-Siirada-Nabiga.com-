Baro Naxwaha (Grammar Rules)

Kahor intaadan bilaabin tartanka, hubi inaad fahamsan tahay isticmaalka Subject Pronouns iyo falka.

Pronoun Verb (Example: Work) Sharaxaad
I / You / We / They Work Falku isma beddelo (Simple form)
He / She / It Works Falka waxaa lagu darayaa -s ama -es

Xusuusnow: "He" waxaa loo isticmaala qof lab ah, "She" qof dheddig ah, "It" shay ama xayawaan, halka "They" loo isticmaalo dad badan.
